I am a third-year PhD student in the [Department of Computer Science](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/dcs/) at the [University of Warwick](https://warwick.ac.uk/), supervised by [Prof. Mike Joy](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/dcs/people/Mike_Joy/). My research falls under the theme of Artificial Intelligence and Human-Centred Computing.

My research focuses on applying Artificial Intelligence to Education, with particular emphasis on text similarity, plagiarism detection using Natural Language Processing, and source code plagiarism detection in computer science education.

- **MSc in Computer Science**, University of Warwick (2021 -- 2022)
